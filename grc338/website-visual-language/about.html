About Us

Corazón de Pan was born from a simple belief: that the sweetest moments in life are the ones we share.

The bakery’s founder, Isaiah Luna, grew up waking to the smell of fresh conchas and bolillos drifting through their grandmother’s kitchen. Every morning, Abuela Rosa would knead dough by hand, humming softly as she sprinkled sugar over her famous pan dulce. She always said the secret ingredient wasn’t in the recipe — it was in the corazón you put into every batch.

Years later, after moving away from home and realizing how deeply those flavors were tied to family, comfort, and culture, Isaiah decided to bring that sense of warmth to the community. What started as weekend baking for friends turned into a dream: a bakery that celebrated tradition while welcoming everyone like family.

In 2025, Corazón de Pan opened its doors for the first time. The goal was never just to sell pastries — it was to create a space filled with aroma, nostalgia, and connection. Every piece of pan dulce is crafted with care, inspired by Abuela’s recipes yet infused with modern creativity. From colorful conchas to seasonal specialties, every pastry carries a story.

Corazón de Pan remains dedicated to honoring the past while baking a sweeter future. We hope that with every bite, you feel the same comfort, joy, and love that first inspired this bakery — because here, everything truly comes from the heart.
